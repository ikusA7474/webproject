Schools for Computer Programming

MIT

MIT is located in Cambridge, MA. Within walking distance of a boba shop, bakery, and greek restaurant. Due to the fact that it is located in Massachusetts, it will require me to fly back and forth between Houston and Cambridge. It is quite scenic, and has a low crime rate of 12.67 per 1,000 students.

Yale University

Yale is located in New Haven, Connecticut. It is in walking distance of a cafe and pizza place. In my free time, I would learn to ice skate at the nearby ice rink. New Haven is very pretty, and it has a crime rate of 14.7 incidents per thousand students.

Bunker Hill Community College

BHCC is located in Boston, MA. It is in walking distance of a restaurant and skating rink, with a crime rate of 1.94 incidents per 1,000 students.

University of Chicago

The University of Chicago is located in Chicago, IL (duh). It is within walking distance of two cafes and a restaurant. It is quite pretty, and has an average crime rate of 7.56 incidents per thousand students.

DePaul University

DePaul University is also located in Chicago, just north of downtown. There are 2 cafes and a restaurant inside the campus, with a diner just outside.
